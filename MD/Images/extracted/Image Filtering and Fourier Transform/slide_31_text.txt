1D FFT
The 1D FFT is a "Divide and Conquer" algorithm that reduces complexity from O(N^2) to O(N log N).
It splits the data into Even and Odd samples, solves them as smaller sub-problems, and then recombines them.
This efficiency relies on the Symmetry and Periodicity of the "Twiddle Factor," which allows the algorithm to reuse previous calculations instead of repeating.
