Types of Noises in Images
Sources of Noise
Capture Noise: Noise in the environment of the scene captured
Lighting (high lit and low lit)
Sensor (temperature fluctuations, electrical interference)
Lens (dust, fog etc.)
Sampling Noise: Continuous Real World Scene to Discrete Pixels. Can cause aliasing effects
Processing Noise: Noise introduced when processing images due to mathematical approximations
Image Compression Noise: JPEG Lossy Compression removes finer details
